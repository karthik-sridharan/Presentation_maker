After deploying:

1. Open `index.html?v=stage36e-20260426-1&clearLuminaStorage=1`.
2. Confirm the app loads with no startup error box.
3. Add a slide, then click Undo; the slide should disappear or return to the previous deck state.
4. Click Redo; the slide should come back.
5. Edit title/body text and press `Ctrl/⌘+Z`; the previous editor text/deck state should be restored.
6. Press `Ctrl/⌘+Shift+Z` or `Ctrl/⌘+Y`; the edit should be redone.
7. Move or resize a figure, then Undo and Redo.
8. Export generated HTML and confirm Stage 36D/36C presentation behavior remains unchanged.
